---
title: Bats
summary: Bats in attics, soffits, walls, or living spaces usually point to a structural entry point that needs exclusion, not just removal.
image: ""
signs:
  - Scratching or fluttering near dusk and dawn
  - Droppings around entry points or attic spaces
  - Strong odor in enclosed areas
handling: Do not block bats inside the structure or handle them bare-handed. If a bat is found in a living space, isolate the room if possible and call for guidance on safe removal and exclusion.
prevention: Schedule a proper exclusion, seal secondary gaps, and correct the exterior entry points that allow colony access.
