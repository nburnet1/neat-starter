---
title: Birds
summary: Bird issues can range from small nesting problems to larger commercial roosting and contamination concerns.
image: ""
signs:
  - Nesting in vents, signs, ledges, or roof transitions
  - Droppings buildup around entries and walkways
  - Repeated roosting on the same structures
handling: Avoid disturbing active nests without a plan. Bird issues usually require exclusion, deterrents, or netting based on the structure and species involved.
prevention: Install the right exclusion materials, close nest-friendly gaps, and use deterrents that fit the building and bird pressure.
