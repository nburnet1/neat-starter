**Possum Removal & Control Services**

Possums are opportunistic marsupials that frequently take up residence under homes, decks, and crawlspaces. While generally non-aggressive, they create unpleasant odors, carry parasites, and can make quite a mess when foraging near living quarters. Our professional possum control services focus on humane trapping, entry point exclusion, and property cleanup.

**Possum Habits & Behavior**

Possums are slow-moving, nocturnal marsupials (pouched mammals) best known for their defense mechanism of feigning death ("playing possum"). They are solitary animals that do not dig their own burrows; instead, they take advantage of existing cavities dug by other animals or open gaps underneath human structures. 

**When Possums Are Active**

**Daily**

* Strictly active during nighttime hours (nocturnal)

**Seasonal**

* Spring & Summer: Most active due to breeding and carrying young in pouches
* Fall: Searching for secure, warm winter den sites
* Winter: Remain active but forage closer to their dens during freezing weather

Possum problems are most noticeable when they seek shelter beneath structures during breeding seasons or cold snaps.

**Where Possums Commonly Cause Damage**

Possums choose low-lying, dark areas that offer immediate shelter and warmth, including:

* Crawlspaces and open areas beneath mobile homes
* Underneath low decks, porches, and garden sheds
* Inside detached garages and outbuildings
* Root cellars and woodpiles
* Low-hanging roof lines or attics with easy tree access

They are often discovered due to heavy scratching noises coming from beneath floors.

**Possum Odor & Contamination Damage**

While they lack the jaw strength to cause massive structural tearing, possums damage property by:

* Defecating and urinating heavily near their den sites, leading to strong, foul odors
* Dying inside tight crawlspaces or wall voids, creating severe carcass odor issues
* Shredding insulation under floors to build crude nests
* Tearing up vapor barriers in crawlspaces

They also carry a high volume of fleas, ticks, and mites, which can easily migrate into living spaces.

**What Possums Eat**

Possums are true omnivores that act as natural scavengers, eating:

* Unsecured garbage, compost piles, and decaying organic matter
* Outdoor pet food and dropped fruit from trees
* Carrion (dead animals) and insects
* Small snakes, rodents, and garden pests
