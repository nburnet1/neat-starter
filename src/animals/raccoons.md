---
title: Raccoons
summary: Raccoons create major damage in attics, chimneys, and crawl spaces and often return when access points stay open.
image: ""
signs:
  - Heavy thumping or movement at night
  - Torn shingles, fascia, or vent covers
  - Strong odor or contaminated insulation
handling: Avoid direct contact and do not corner or approach a raccoon on the property. Problem raccoons need a controlled removal plan, especially around young animals.
prevention: Secure roof and crawl space openings, repair damage quickly, and remove repeated access opportunities around the structure.
---
