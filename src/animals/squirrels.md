---
title: Squirrels
summary: Squirrels often chew into soffits, roof returns, vents, and other weak points, especially around attics and crawl spaces.
image: ""
signs:
  - Running or chewing sounds during daylight hours
  - Roofline damage or disturbed vents
  - Insulation disturbance in attic spaces
handling: Do not trap squirrels inside walls or close entry holes while animals are still active inside. Confirm activity and create a removal plan first.
prevention: Repair roofline damage, secure vents, trim back access routes, and seal identified entry points after removal.
---
