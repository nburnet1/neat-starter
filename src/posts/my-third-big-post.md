Wildlife removal is only one part of a complete solution. When the damaged area stays open, the same type of animal often comes back.

## Common repair areas

- Soffits and fascia
- Roof returns and roof transitions
- Vent covers and screens
- Crawl space and chimney access points

## Why repair matters

Exterior repairs, cleanup, and exclusion work help protect the structure and reduce the chance of repeated wildlife entry.
