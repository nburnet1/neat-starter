Squirrels are tough on roof returns, soffits, vents, and weak transition points. Once they find an entry route, they often keep using it until the structure is repaired.

## Signs of squirrel activity

- Fast movement during daylight hours
- Chewing sounds near soffits or walls
- Torn vents or disturbed roof edges
- Insulation disruption inside attic spaces

## Prevention steps

After removal, repair the damaged entry point, secure vents, and address tree access or other routes that make the structure easy to reach.
