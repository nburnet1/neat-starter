Bird problems can build up slowly and then become expensive. Ledges, roof transitions, signs, vents, and commercial structures often need more than basic cleanup.

## When to call

If birds keep returning to the same location, if droppings are building up, or if nesting is affecting access points, it is time to look at exclusion or deterrent work.

## Long-term prevention

The right solution depends on the building. Netting, exclusion materials, and species-specific deterrents work best when matched to the structure and pressure.
